# Privacy Policy

`yamlification` is a local plugin package containing prompt-based skills and static assets.

You can use it with your your AI agent / harness of choice.

It does not include its own remote service, telemetry pipeline, or account system.
Any data handling is performed by the host product where the skills are installed.

This repository does not intentionally collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## Host Platform Data

When used inside Claude, Codex, or another compatible host, prompts, files, and outputs
may be processed according to that host platform's policies and your configuration.
